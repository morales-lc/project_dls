## Scoring Instructions

To calculate the SUS score:

1. For **odd-numbered questions** (1, 3, 5, 7, 9): Subtract 1 from the user response
2. For **even-numbered questions** (2, 4, 6, 8, 10): Subtract the user response from 5
3. Sum all the converted responses and multiply by 2.5

**Formula:** SUS Score = Sum of converted responses × 2.5

The final score will range from 0 to 100.

### Score Interpretation:
- **80.3 or higher:** Excellent (Grade A)
- **68 - 80.2:** Good (Grade B)
- **68:** Average/Acceptable (Grade C)
- **51 - 67.9:** Poor (Grade D)
- **Below 51:** Awful (Grade F)

**Note:** A score above 68 is considered above average. The average SUS score across all systems is approximately 68.
