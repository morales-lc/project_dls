Learning Spaces



Collaboratory


There are 2 collaborative learning spaces available for student discussions, project meetings, and work sessions. Reservations for their use are made at the Concierge. Food and drinks are not allowed in the rooms.



Graduate Thesis Room


Three rooms are available to Graduate students who are doing their thesis writing.



Individual Study Carrels


There are 27 study carrels for individual study available to students. Reservations for use are made at the Concierge.

Seminarraum


The seminar room can be used for administrative meetings; seminars, workshops and other activities in support of academic and/or co-curricular activities. Seating is for 12 around a table, plus 8 extra seats.



Tutorium


The faculty can use the Faculty Room for mentoring, tutoring, or counseling purposes.
